ENROLMENT STANDARDS

Dear Parents and Prospective Students

We are very pleased to welcome you to our community at CIA FIRST International School.

Our school accepts students from ages 3 to 18 regardless of race, gender, status or religious affiliation.


We provide an authentic and challenging program with an exciting range of

activities to ensure students of all ages feel energised, engaged and empowered.


Whether you are making a transition from another country or another local international school,

we work in partnership with parents to ensure your child felt safe, happy and cared for.

Enrollment procedure

1. Visit CIA FIRST International School. You may call 012/095/099 2000 11 to make an appointment or send us an email to info@ciaschool.edu.kh.

2. Complete the form that you will receive during your school visit.

3. Schedule your child’s testing day.

4. Meet with an administration and academic representatives to review testing results and complete enrollment.

Documents needed

1. Birth Certificate / Passport

2. Birth Certificate is required for sibling discount calculation

3. Previous school report cards/transcript

4. Official transfer document for Khmer Curriculum Elective

5. Two 4 x 6 cm coloured photographs to the enrollment form. (Individual photographs of the student, the mother and the father.)

Admission to Grade School

A new student must pass an entrance test before acceptance to any level (see below)


• A student could be placed at a level lower than expected for his age-group for the following reasons:

• Poor result of the entrance test or

• Lack of readiness for the lessons at the expected level or

• An ESL student who, even though his/her age is right for the above grade level, has an entrance test result showing this to be necessary.
